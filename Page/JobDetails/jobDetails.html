Are you passionate about helping people?

Do you have an obsession with details and a commitment to getting things done?

Looking for that right opportunity to make a difference and launch your career?

Sound like we're talking to you? We are.

Call us client-focused, client-centric or client-obsessed, we are absolutely committed to our clients' success. We're proud of the long and deep partnerships we've built with our clients and we're looking to expand our team with people ready to take client support to the next level. We help companies compete for talent. We help our clients recruit top talent by using Jobing.com, the leading locally focused job board network and Recruiting.com Sites, the best career sites on the internet.

In this role, you will support clients with their recruiting efforts through use of our products and services. Think of this role as the perfect mixture of client service and sales support.

If you're looking for a standard schedule from day to day, this job isn't for you. We need someone ready to step up and do whatever it takes. Flex your writing skills by drafting a version of a job posting that leverages industry specific keywords to drive better SEO. Return a client's call and answer whatever product or recruiting questions they might throw your way. Be a key player in managing a client's recruitment advertising needs.

What can you expect?

You'll grow here, we'll push and stretch you, and we'll expect you to be your very best-- every day. Because what you'll do here makes a big difference. Your success is based on our clients' success and our business results.

What do we expect?

  • You'll work hard to “wow” our clients and deliver exceptional service.
  • Be available and responsive (after all, we live in a mobile world where being "offline" is a thing of the past).
  • Meet and work with interesting and awesome people - team members and clients.
  • Play an instrumental role in your own growth and in growing our organization.

Skills / Requirements

  • Organization and attention to detail
  • Strong written and non-written communication skills
  • A proven ability to provide exceptional service
  • Previous experience in Marketing/PR or Recruiting is a plus
  • BA/BS degree preferred
